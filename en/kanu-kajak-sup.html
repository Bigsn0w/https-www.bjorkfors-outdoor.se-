Canoes, kayaks & SUPs

Still water, three ways to paddle off.

Two lakes, one jetty, full equipment — all you choose is what to set off in. And if you would rather paddle elsewhere: we also deliver to surrounding lakes.

Booking request See rental prices

01

The canoe

The classic for shared tours: our three-seater canoes carry provisions, fishing gear and conversations alike. Full equipment included — from paddles to life jackets.

  • ✦Three-seater — ideal for families and small groups
  • ✦For evening paddles and full-day tours
  • ✦Introduction for beginners before you head out onto the water

02

The kayak

Agile, fast and close to the water: in our single and double kayaks you explore bays and reed edges no one else can reach — most beautiful in the morning, when the lake is still mirror-calm.

  • ✦Single and double kayaks with full equipment
  • ✦Perfect for Björkern and Åsunden
  • ✦On request we deliver to surrounding lakes

03

The SUP board

Still water, wide bays: stand-up paddling straight from the jetty — the slowest and most beautiful way to explore lake Björkern. Boards are rented by the day — simply step on and push off.

  • ✦Boards by the day, right at the jetty
  • ✦Quiet swimming bays within paddling distance
  • ✦Quick to learn, even for beginners
