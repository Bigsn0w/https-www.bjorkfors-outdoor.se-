Björkern & Åsunden — Östergötland

Your boat. Your waters.

Fully equipped fishing boats, clear fixed prices and hosts who fish themselves — your fishing day starts five hundred metres from the jetty.

The water

Two lakes. Countless drop-offs.

Björkern and Åsunden are right outside the door — clear, deep water with reed edges, rock banks and holes where pike, perch and zander hold. If you want to push your boat into the water at five in the morning, all it takes is a 500-metre walk.

Fishing permits are available directly from us. And if you want to read the water faster, book an experienced fishing guide — he knows the drop-offs no map will show you. Since this year we also work together with Rydaholm Hunting & Fishing, supporting each other in fishing and hunting.
