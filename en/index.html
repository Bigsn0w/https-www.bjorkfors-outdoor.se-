Why Björkfors

We do things differently.

No mass tourism, no hidden costs — and full privacy on the water. Just you, the lake and hosts who fish themselves.

We are hosts by passion — and anglers by conviction. In Björkfors, between lakes Björkern and Åsunden, we run a bed & breakfast for adult groups looking for real nature instead of an off-the-shelf programme. Personal, uncomplicated, with clear fixed prices.

  1. 01

    Full privacy on board

    Each boat is reserved entirely for you on your day — you enjoy the water in your own company and decide for yourselves when to set off, where to fish and for how long. And if you would like tips on the waters, we are happy to share our knowledge — first-hand.

  2. 02

    Our own fleet, ready to go

    Two fully equipped aluminium fishing boats with 20 hp outboards and fish finders, plus a Rana 14 — serviced, moored, five minutes from the house.

  3. 03

    The price is the price

    Every offer carries its fixed price right on the boat, the tour, the room. No surcharges, no fine print.
