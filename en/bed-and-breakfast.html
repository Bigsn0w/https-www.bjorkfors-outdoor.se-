Bed & breakfast

Arrive like coming home.

Our historic B&B on Gamla landsvägen is a base camp, not a hotel: ten cosy bedrooms, a fully equipped guest kitchen and a fire pit under the starry sky — five hundred metres from the water.

Booking request See room prices

Glimpses — rooms, canteen & common areas

01

The rooms

Ten bedrooms in the historic main building — cosy, bright and pared down to the essentials. Plus a wood stove on the first floor for cold evenings and clean, fresh sanitary facilities.

02

Kitchen & breakfast

A breakfast that carries you through a long day on the water — with a packed lunch for the boat on request. Cook for yourselves in the fully equipped guest kitchen, or let our private kitchen prepare and serve delicious meals for you on request.

03

Canteen, BBQ & fire pit

Long tables for long evenings: the guest canteen is where stories are told, meals are shared and plans are made. Outside, barbecue facilities await the catch of the day — and afterwards the evening winds down at the fire pit.

For groups

The Allhus — room for sixty.

Club outing, fishing club or company weekend: the Allhus seats up to 60 people in one place — for shared meals, meetings or the evening after the catch.
