House rules

To keep things pleasant for everyone, we ask you to keep the following in mind. Questions about your stay? Feel free to get in touch.

  1. 01

    Shoes & Cleanliness

    • Take off your shoes at the entrance; use the shoe racks.
    • Do not dry outdoor boots, fishing boots or wet clothing in your room.
    • Keep rooms and common areas tidy.
  2. 02

    Damage & Duty to Report

    • Please report immediately if something is broken or not working properly.
    • Unreported damage may be charged afterwards.
    • Improper use of the facilities may cause additional costs.
  3. 03

    Noise & Quiet

    • Avoid excessive noise, both indoors and outdoors.
    • Quiet hours from 11pm.
    • Respect other guests and nature.
  4. 04

    Food & Canteen

    • Do not eat in the upper floors; use the canteen or outdoor area.
    • Leave the guest kitchen and canteen clean.
    • Turn off the lights when leaving the kitchen, canteen and common areas.
  5. 05

    Energy & Lighting

    • Turn off the lights when leaving your room, the shower and the toilet.
    • Use water and electricity mindfully.
  6. 06

    Waste & Environment

    • Do not leave any waste on the premises.
    • Sort waste according to the marked bins.
    • Respect the flora and fauna around the B&B.
  1. 07

    Safety

    • Smoking only in designated areas.
    • No open fire or candles in or near the rooms.
    • Follow the instructions for boat use, trailer transport and outdoor activities.
  2. 08

    Boat & Fishing Rules

    • Luxury fishing boats are only available to guests with a reservation and experience.
    • Catch & release according to local regulations.
    • Remember your personal fishing licence.
    • Trailer use only under the supervision of our team.
    • Damage to boats and engines caused by improper use may be charged to the renter.
  3. 09

    Pets

    • Only allowed with prior consent.
    • Always kept on a leash on the premises.
    • Please clean up after your pet immediately.
    • Not allowed on sofas or beds!
    • If it turns out after departure that pets have been on sofas or beds, additional cleaning costs will be charged.
    • Must not cause any nuisance.
  4. 10

    Visitors

    • External visitors only by prior arrangement.
    • No unauthorised guests in rooms or on the premises.
  5. 11

    Liability

    • Björkfors Outdoor Adventures is not liable for loss of or damage to personal belongings.
    • Participation in outdoor activities is at your own risk, unless stated otherwise.
